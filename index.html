Course Ideas Behind This Journal

This journal is based on the concepts from the Learning How to Learn course. Here is how it applies the theory to practice:

Course idea How this journal uses it
Focused Mode The Daily Learning Report asks what the learner did during focused work.
Diffuse Mode The journal asks how the learner switched attention after focused work.
Recall The learner writes three ideas they can recall without notes.
Chunking The learner creates one “chunk of the day”.
Process over Product The learner plans small 10–25 minute process steps.
Procrastination The Anti-Procrastination Page helps identify feelings, triggers, and a next action.
Motivation and Reward The journal includes rewards for consistency and completed study steps.
Illusions of Competence The learner compares perceived understanding with tested understanding.
